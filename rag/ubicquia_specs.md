# Ubicquia Product Specifications for Smart City Deployment

## UbiCell (Intelligent Streetlight Control)
*   **Energy Savings:** Up to 40% reduction in energy usage through intelligent scheduling and dimming (0-10V, DALI).
*   **Metering:** Utility-grade metering (0.5% accuracy) for precise billing and load monitoring.
*   **Resilience:** Integrated tilt and vibration sensors to detect pole knockdowns or storm damage.
*   **Connectivity:** LTE CAT-1/M1/NB-IoT backhaul.
*   **Installation:** NEMA 7-pin plug-and-play.
*   **Sensors:** Voltage, Current, Power Factor, GPS, Tilt/Vibration.

## UbiHub (AP/AI & AI+)
*   **Edge AI:** Features NVIDIA Jetson (AI+) or Qualcomm (AP6) processors for privacy-preserving edge analytics.
*   **Public Safety:** Dual 4K cameras for situational awareness. License Plate Recognition (LPR) capable.
*   **Connectivity:** WiFi 6 (802.11ax) Access Point for public internet or digital divide initiatives. 3rd 5GHz radio for mesh backhaul.
*   **Form Factor:** Plugs into the photocell socket; minimal visual clutter ("zero footprint"). Replaces existing photocell.
*   **Privacy:** Edge processing ensures video doesn't need to be streamed to the cloud unless an event triggers it. Support for privacy masking.

## UbiGrid (DTM+)
*   **Grid Monitoring:** Real-time transformer monitoring (oil temp, pressure, vibration).
*   **Power Quality:** Sags, swells, and harmonic distortion analysis.
*   **Theft Detection:** Detects copper theft via unique vibration/power signatures.
*   **Installation:** Magnetic mount or pole mount; compatible with existing transformers.
*   **Specs:** IP67 rated, 10kV surge protection.
